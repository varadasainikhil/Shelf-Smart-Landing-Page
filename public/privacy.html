This Privacy Notice for ShelfSmart ("we," "us," or "our"), describes how and why we might access, collect, store, use, and/or share ("process") your personal information when you use our services ("Services"), including when you:

  • Use ShelfSmart. ShelfSmart is an iOS application designed for grocery management and recipe discovery. Core Functionalities: Inventory and Expiry Tracking: Users can log grocery products and their corresponding expiration dates. The application sends notifications to the user as the expiration dates approach. Inventory-Based Recipe Suggestions: Based on the items logged in the user's inventory, the app provides recipe suggestions that utilize those ingredients, particularly those nearing their expiration date. Recipe Search and Filtering: A dedicated recipe search function allows users to find recipes independently of their current inventory. This search functionality includes filters for various preferences, including: Dietary restrictions (e.g., vegan, gluten-free) Cuisine type (e.g., Italian, Mexican, Japanese) Common allergens (e.g., nuts, dairy, soy) Meal type (e.g., breakfast, dinner) Data Storage and Handling: User data is handled in two distinct ways based on its purpose: Application Data: All user-generated content related to the app's core function—such as grocery lists, recipes, and preferences—is stored locally on the user's device. This data can be synchronized across the user's devices via their personal iCloud account. Authentication Data: User account creation and login are managed by Google's Firebase Authentication service. This service stores the data necessary for authentication, which includes the user's name, email address, and sign-up method. Monetization Model: The application is available free of charge and does not include in-app purchases or subscription fees.
